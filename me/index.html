the dude

25 years of age, living in Deerfield MA--Pioneer Valley is home.

Grew up in central MA, went to a decent school with a lot of talent.

Never felt like I fit in (and who did?), held a variety of pursuits.

Started making music when I was 19, been at it ever since.

I've felt a pull my young adulthood, a drive to be something bigger.

But sadly, I've always had a bit of an attention deficit.

Great with ideas and imagination, the practical side never seems to

follow through.


So, here I am, attempting to document my mind, the chaos that it is,

as it continues its pursuit of so many different things,

perpetually in divide.


Thanks for checking in.
