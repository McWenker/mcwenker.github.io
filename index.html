Welcome

In a world that seems to get smaller every day, it's funny to think that we are in a state of constant expansion. Our universe is larger this second than the last... and as far as we can tell, that isn't changing anytime soon. So, no use falling behind!

My name is Tyler, and I am a 25-year-old self-taught web developer from Massachusetts (and soon to be traveling!). I have been messing around with HTML and CSS since the days of MySpace and Digg. I have dabbled and experimented with Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere, After Effects, and a myriad of their analogs since 2001. Since 2009 I have worked with a variety of audio recording and production equipment for my own personal music project, and have had the pleasure of interning at a handful of recording studios. I have been an avid video gamer since before I can really remember... and despite two decades and half of exposure, I have often struggled with technology. I know many of us have, and know how frustrating it can be to find oneself caught up in a whirlwind of change that seems to occur every other week!.

So I offer my services to you, dear reader. From web development to printer setup, from cloud usage to animation design, I am ready and willing to help you with your technology problem(s)! Despite my past frustrations, I am proficient in the following:

  • Website Development (HTML/CSS, PHP, CFML)
  • Music Production (Music, Foley Design, Mixing, etc.)
  • Graphic Design (Logos/Branding, Photo Manipulation, 2D Animation, etc.)
  • Tech Support (Email Troubleshooting, Software-Specifics, Hardware Setup, SEO, etc.)
  • Much More (Copy-Editing, Organizational Management, Newslettering, etc...)

Whatever the project, I will happily discuss possibilities with you - at no cost - to see if I am the right fit for your project, your issue, or your vision. All consultations are done in-person (or over phone/Skype for long-distance).
