Obligatory first post

April 14, 2016 seizer

So over the past month or so I've put together a couple of songs. None of them are super polished (per my usual nature), but they were all fun as hell. One in particular I'm very pleased with the sound of the instrumentation:

I even put down some goofy auto-tuned vocals over it:

If I were a better singer, I'd say I'm pretty attached to both. The vocals were totally free-styled though, so that was a fun experience. I liked the message even if it ended up being a little spun-out.

I also made a really disjointed beat that's sort of reminiscient of Tycho, but also Pretty Lights and maybe Bonobo? It never really got ground down to a defining message, hence its short length:

And then finally, another goofy vocal track. The backing instrumentation of this felt awesome, I really liked the sickening sweetness of it. The sound of the vocals was borderline obnoxious, but that's why the lyrics got so absurd. When it all comes together, I get a really nice 80s shopping mall vibe from it:

I'm making beats all the time, but not too many make it online. I'm always getting closer to a place where I feel comfortable compiling an album or EP, but who knows what the timeline on that is these days.

Thanks for listening!
