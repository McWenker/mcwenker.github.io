OMNISLASH

March 9, 2016 Tyler

I am very, very pleased to present my first ever, full-length digital release: OMNISLASH.

Available on Bandcamp.

It's inspired by Sweet Valley's Eternal Champ, Nujabes, BLANK BANSHEE, wavemob, and many more. It samples a slew of video games from the early 90s, including Final Fantasy, Chrono Trigger, and Zelda.

I've been working on bits and pieces of this album for nearly the entirety of this trip (although, some parts of it were made back even before RIVERSIDE_FM), and am so happy to finally have it out there. I'm even happier to have gotten great reviews from both friends and family, and strangers! I'd be thrilled if you checked it out.
