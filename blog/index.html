Days in the life

Over the entirety of 2016, my partner and I were ground down by the monotony of the working life.
So we decided try a different lifestyle. If you go on social media of any kind, I'm sure you've seen the people living out of vans, busses, RVs, what have you.
We felt inspired.

I converted my Hyundai Santa Fe to fit a mattress into backseat, and we readied ourselves for a life on the road.
It was smaller than the fantasy lives we saw in the screens, but it was true and it was our own.

After all, this is our story.
