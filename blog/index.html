Project Santa Fe

Over the past year (2016), my partner and I have been ground down by the monotony of the working life. You know the drill: wake up, go to work, come home, feel too tired for much of anything, repeat. So, we decided to do something different. If you go on social media of any kind, I'm sure you've seen the people living out of vans, busses, RVs, what have you.

I converted my Hyundai Santa Fe to fit a mattress into backseat, and we readied ourselves for a life on the road. We've come to jokingly refer to it as "Project Santa Fe."

This is our story.
