Watermelon Wednesdays

Celebrating 17 Years of the Finest Acoustic Music

At the West Whately Chapel near the intersection of Conway and Williamsburg Roads. CLICK HERE for directions.
Nearly all shows are on Wednesdays and begin at 7:30PM. Doors open at 7:15PM, line forms earlier.
Online tickets are highly recommended!
